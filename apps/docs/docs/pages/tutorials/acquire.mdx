# Acquire an ad space

![Acquire](/tutorials/group-page.jpg)

Once you have signed in and find yourself on a ad group page, you can browse all ad spaces
with their ad data and price. Click on the one you'd like to purchase and you'll find yourself
on the ad space page.

Hit acquire space to open up the purchse modal.
![Ad](/tutorials/ad-page.jpg)

:::note
Note:
If you want to buy an ad space, make sure you fund you smart account with tokens from
the ad space currency. You can find you smart account address in your profile page.
:::

Steps:
1. Wrap SuperTokens needed for the tax stream
2. Click on the "Acquire space" button.
