# Sign in

AdLand uses smart accounts for all the interactions with the protocol. In order to interact with the protocol, you can either
sign in with your
- Farcaster account
- Wallet

Click the top right corner of the page to sign in.

![SignIn](/tutorials/signin.jpg)
<br />

Once you're signed in, you'll be able to get your account address in your profile page but clicking the top right corner of the page.
